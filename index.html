Who we are

About Us

Welcome to Beer and Burger Joint, Auckland CBD's premier destination for mouthwatering burgers and carefully selected craft beers. Located at the iconic Prince Wharf on Quay Street, we bring you the perfect combination of quality food and great atmosphere.

Whether you're looking for a quick takeaway or a relaxed dine-in experience, we've got you covered. Our commitment to using premium ingredients and supporting local breweries ensures every visit is memorable. Join us at Auckland's Viaduct and taste the difference!
